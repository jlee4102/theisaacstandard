05 Usage guidelines

DO
  • Use the mark on a contrasting background — ink on cream, mark-light on dark.
  • Give it room — at least the height of the mark as clearspace on all sides.
  • Use the SVG whenever possible for crisp scaling.
  • Keep the wordmark italic on "Standard" — it's part of the brand.
DON'T
  • Recolor the mark or wordmark to colors outside the palette.
  • Squish, stretch, rotate, or skew the logo.
  • Drop shadows, glows, or 3D effects on the mark.
  • Place over busy photography without a dark overlay or scrim.
